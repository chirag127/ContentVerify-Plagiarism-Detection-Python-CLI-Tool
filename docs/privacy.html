Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use Plagiarism-checker-Python, a local Python script designed to detect plagiarism in textual documents using cosine similarity. This tool is developed and maintained by Chirag Singhal.

1. Information We Collect

Plagiarism-checker-Python is a standalone script that runs entirely on your local machine. As such, no data is sent to any server or external service. The only information processed is the text content you provide for plagiarism analysis, and this data remains on your device at all times.

2. How We Use Your Information

The text input you provide is used solely for the purpose of computing cosine similarity between documents to detect potential plagiarism. All processing occurs locally. No logs, copies, or metadata are stored, shared, or transmitted beyond your machine.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Since no data is transmitted from your device, there is no sharing, selling, or disclosure of your content to any third parties. You retain full ownership and control over your documents and text inputs.

4. Security

As the application runs locally, the security of your data depends on your own system’s protections. We recommend using antivirus software and keeping your operating system updated. The script itself does not access the internet or external storage unless explicitly configured by you.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. Since this is an open-source tool, changes will also be visible in the repository's commit history.
